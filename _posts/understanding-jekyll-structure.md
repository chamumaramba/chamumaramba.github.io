A Jekyll project follows a specific structure that helps organize content and layouts.

### Important Folders:
- `_layouts` – contains page templates
- `_posts` – stores blog posts
- `_config.yml` – configuration file for the site

### Why Structure Matters:
Having a clear structure makes it easier to manage and scale your website as it grows.

Through this project, I gained a better understanding of how static websites are organized and maintained.
